06  /  10
Honesty over fluency.

When she doesn't know,
she says so.

i.

She begins each day knowing nothing.

Mia carries no opinions about you, no assumptions, no defaults. Only what's been written down on her behalf.

ii.

Every fact has a source.

If she names a person, a date, a dose — it came from something a person told her. Never from a guess.

iii.

Silence becomes honesty.

When she doesn't have the answer, she says so: "I don't have that in my memory yet — your family can help me add it."
